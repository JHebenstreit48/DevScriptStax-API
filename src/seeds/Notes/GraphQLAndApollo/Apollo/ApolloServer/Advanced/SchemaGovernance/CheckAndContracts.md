## Schema Governance (Checks and Contracts)

---

### Overview

Schema governance is about keeping a GraphQL schema safe, stable, and maintainable as it evolves.

This often includes validation, compatibility checks, and coordination across teams.

---

### Apollo Federation (High-Level)

Federation is an approach to composing a larger graph from multiple services.

This topic is often related to governance because it impacts:

- ownership boundaries
- schema composition
- change management

---

### Schema Stitching (High-Level)

Schema stitching is another approach for composing schemas.

It can be useful in certain situations but typically introduces complexity that governance processes need to manage.
